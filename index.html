THANK YOU!

A huge thank you for your interest in Tampa Bay Conservation Society. We have many exciting events happening, below is some of our highlighted programs and events. We are constantly adding new content and programs to our website, to stay up to date feel free to visit us at  TampaBayConservationSociety.org
February 10th 7:30 pm at Jan Platt Library we are having our first free quarterly public outreach meeting. Dr. Taegan McMahon, a local amphibian conservationist, will talk about her research that has been published in National Geographic. 
TBCSoc is out and about with the scouts. We are now offering 12 different Girl Scout workshops and 12 different Boy Scout workshops, filled with hands on environmental adventures. 
Whether it's a dapper adult soiree or a super hero birthday, our program animals will come to you.  Visit the website for more information about booking.
